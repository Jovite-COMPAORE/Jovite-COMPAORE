I'm a **statistician and data analyst** specialized in modeling and visualizing complex datasets. I extract actionable insights from data - especially in sectors with heavy logistics and public-sector challenges.

- I've trained public servants at **INSD**, **DGESS (Ministry of Economy & Finance)** and **Direction Générale des Impôts** on statistical tools
- Fun side project: I built an **automated news briefing bot** running twice a day with Llama 3.3 and Telegram
- Beyond code: passionate about **West African economic data** and Sahel geopolitics
- **Scholar** of the World Bank and the PHASAO project (2019–2025)
